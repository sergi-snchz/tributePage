Kawhi Leonard

The man that took two franchises to the promised land. And is now leading the Los Angeles Clippers.

Kawhi Leonard taking the game winning shot in game 7 at the 2018 Eastern Conference Semifinals.

Here is a brief timeline of Kawhi Leonard

  • 1991 - Born in Los Angeles, California
  • 2009 - Graduated high school, as a four-star recruit
  • 2009 - 2010 - Played for San Diego State University. As a freshman Leonard averaged 12.7 points and 9.9 rebounds per game
  • 2010 - 2011 - As a sophomore Leonard averaged 15.7 points and 10.4 rebounds per game
  • 2011 - Leaves San Diego State to enter the 2011 NBA draft
  • 2011 - Was selected with the 15th overall pick by the Indiana Pacers but was traded that night to the San Antonio Spurs
  • 2013 - The San Antonio Spurs reached the NBA Finals to play versus the Miami Heat. Leonard averaged 14.6 points and 11.1 rebounds but the Spurs lost the series in seven games
  • 2014 - Once again the Spurs and Heat met in the NBA finals. This time Leonard averaged 17.8 points per game on 61% shooting. The Spurs beat the Heat in 5 games. Leonard was named NBA Finals MVP.
  • 2015 - Was named Defensive Player of Year
  • 2017 - In the third quarter of game one versus the Golden State Warriors. Leonard injured his ankle
  • 2017 - 2018 - Only played ten games because of multiple injuries
  • 2018 - Was traded along teammate Danny Green to the Toronto Raptors in exchange for DeMar DeRozan, Jakob poltl and a protected 2019 first-round draft pick
  • 2019 - Hit the game winner in game seven versus the Philadelphia 76ers from the corner at the buzzer, to advance to the Eastern Conference Finals
  • 2019 - Won his second NBA Championship. This time with the Toronto Raptors. Leonard was named Finals MVP
  • 2019 - Signed a three-year $103 million contract with the Los Angeles Clippers
